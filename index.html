Frequently asked questions

How do I get licensed as a cook?
We guide you through the process: upload your ID, food handler's permit, and kitchen photos. We'll schedule a virtual inspection and help you meet local regulations.
What are the fees for cooks?
We take a 10% platform fee on each order. No listing fees or monthly charges.
Is my food safe?
Every kitchen is licensed and inspected. Cooks submit daily temperature and cleaning logs. We monitor compliance continuously.
Can I order delivery?
Yes, if the cook offers delivery. You'll see delivery options at checkout. Pickup is always available.
What if something goes wrong with my order?
We have a dispute resolution team. Your payment is held until you confirm receipt, so you're protected.
