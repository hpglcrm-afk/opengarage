Why Open Garage? Because it solves real problems.

Not a fancy app – a practical business platform.

For cooks: A way to earn without the restaurant grind

  • The problem: You love cooking but can't afford a restaurant. Regulations are confusing. You're stuck at home.
  • Our solution: We handle licensing, compliance, and payments. You just cook. Your kitchen becomes a legit business.

For customers: Real food from real people

  • The problem: Delivery apps are expensive, impersonal, and full of chain restaurants. You want authentic homemade meals.
  • Our solution: Order directly from licensed home cooks in your neighborhood. Support local families and enjoy real cooking.

For communities: A resilient local food network

  • The problem: Centralized food systems are fragile. One disruption affects thousands.
  • Our solution: Hundreds of small kitchens spread risk, create local jobs, and keep money in the neighborhood.

Trust & safety: We take it seriously

  • The problem: Is it safe to eat from a home kitchen? Are they following rules?
  • Our solution: Every cook is licensed, insured, and monitored. Daily temperature logs, inspections, and customer ratings ensure quality.

Frequently asked questions

How do I get licensed as a cook?
We guide you through the process: upload your ID, food handler's permit, and kitchen photos. We'll schedule a virtual inspection and help you meet local regulations.
What are the fees for cooks?
We take a 10% platform fee on each order. There are no listing fees or monthly charges. You keep the rest.
Is my food safe?
Every kitchen is licensed and inspected. Cooks submit daily temperature and cleaning logs. We monitor compliance continuously.
How do I get my food?
You can choose pickup or delivery (if the cook offers it). We'll send you real-time updates and the cook's contact info.
